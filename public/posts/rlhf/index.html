References

Stable Literature 1.0

  1. Nathan Lambert’s RLHF book, a valiant attempt to capture a “stable literature” in an evolving LLM post-training battlefield
  2. Kevin Murphy’s Reinforcement Learning: An Overview 👉 Chapter 6 talks about RL & LLM
  3. Sutton and Barto’s Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction (2nd Edition) 👉 the OG RL book updated with new stuff
  4. Norvig and Russell’s Artificial Intelligence: A Modern Approach 👉 Berkeley CS 188 textbook & the most popular one in the world — an overview of intelligent agents with a heavy emphasis on RL
  5. Meta’s comprehensive lit review on RL algorithms for LLMs 👉 Understanding Reinforcement Learning for Model Training, and future directions with GRAPE (2025) by Patel, arXiv.
  6. Extensive review on reasoning models 👉 A Survey of Reinforcement Learning for Large Reasoning Models (2025) by Zhang et al., arXiv.

Evolving Literature

RLHF for Language Models

  1. The hottest AI startup Thinking Machines sells post-training as a service 👉 product: Tinker; blog: LoRA Without Regret
  2. Cursor’s blogpost on their wickedly effective RL models 👉 Improving Cursor Tab with online RL
  3. Nathan Lambert’s blogpost Interconnects 👉 frequently writes about reasoning models, agents, post-training, etc.
  4. Hugging Face’s post-training course smol-course 👉 hands-on course on language model fine-tuning basics
  5. Fei-Fei Li and team’s AI agent paper 👉 Agent AI: Surveying the Horizons of Multimodal Interaction (2024) by Durante et al., arXiv.
  6. NVIDIA uses RL in pretraining 👉 RLP: Reinforcement as a Pretraining Objective (2025) by Hatamizadeh et al., arXiv.
  7. Meta’s new self-play training strategy for Llama 👉 Language Self-Play For Data-Free Training (2025) by Kuba et al., arXiv.

RLFH for RecSys

  1. OneRec-V2 improves Generative Recommendation post-training 👉 OneRec-V2 Technical Report (2025) by Zhou et al., arXiv.
  2. Shopee’s OnePiece integrates RL with traditional cascade RecSys 👉 OnePiece: Bringing Context Engineering and Reasoning to Industrial Cascade Ranking System (2025) by Dai et al., arXiv.
  3. Douyin improves user understanding with reasoning 👉 Conf-Profile: A Confidence-Driven Reasoning Paradigm for Label-Free User Profiling (2025) by Li et al., arXiv.
  4. Pinterest uses RL to tune value models of multi-objective rankers 👉 Deep Reinforcement Learning for Ranking Utility Tuning in the Ad Recommender System at Pinterest (2025) by Xiao et al., arXiv.
